Cooking Guide

Perfect Cooking Every Time

Follow our simple instructions to enjoy restaurant-quality meals at home

Microwave Instructions

For quick and convenient meals, the microwave is your best friend.

1
Remove packaging and place on microwave-safe plate
2
Cover with microwave-safe lid
3
Heat on high for 3-5 minutes

Oven Instructions

For crispy, golden results.

1
Preheat oven to 375F (190C)
2
Place on baking sheet
3
Bake for 15-25 minutes

Stovetop Instructions

For precise control and delicious results.

1
Heat non-stick pan over medium heat
2
Add oil or butter if desired
3
Cook for 5-8 minutes per side

Air Fryer Instructions

For crispy results with less oil.

1
Preheat to 375F (190C)
2
Place in basket
3
Cook for 10-15 minutes
