Cooking Guide

Perfect Cooking Every Time

Follow our simple instructions to enjoy restaurant-quality meals at home

Microwave Instructions

For quick and convenient meals, the microwave is your best friend. Follow these steps for perfect results:

1
Remove packaging and place the food on a microwave-safe plate
2
Cover with a microwave-safe lid or paper towel to prevent splattering
3
Heat on high power for 3-5 minutes, depending on the item
4
Let stand for 1-2 minutes before serving

Pro Tips:

For best results, rotate the dish halfway through cooking. If your microwave doesn't have a turntable, manually rotate the dish 180 degrees.

Oven Instructions

For crispy, golden results, nothing beats an oven. Follow these steps for perfect results:

1
Preheat your oven to 375F (190C)
2
Remove packaging and place the food on a baking sheet
3
Bake for 15-25 minutes, depending on the item
4
Let stand for 2-3 minutes before serving

Pro Tips:

For extra crispiness, place the food directly on the oven rack with a baking sheet below to catch any drips.

Stovetop Instructions

For precise control and delicious results, the stovetop is ideal. Follow these steps for perfect results:

1
Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat
2
Add a small amount of oil or butter if desired
3
Cook for 5-8 minutes per side, depending on the item
4
Ensure internal temperature reaches 165F (74C)

Pro Tips:

Don't overcrowd the pan. Cook in batches if necessary to ensure even browning and cooking.

Air Fryer Instructions

For crispy results with less oil, the air fryer is perfect. Follow these steps for perfect results:

1
Preheat your air fryer to 375F (190C)
2
Remove packaging and place the food in the basket
3
Cook for 10-15 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through
4
Let stand for 1-2 minutes before serving

Pro Tips:

Don't overcrowd the basket. Cook in batches if necessary to ensure even cooking and browning.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How long do your frozen meals last in the freezer? +
Our frozen meals are designed to maintain their quality for up to 6 months when stored properly at 0F (-18C) or below. For best taste and texture, we recommend consuming within 3-4 months of purchase. Always check the "best by" date on the packaging.
Are your ingredients natural and preservative-free? +
Yes, we pride ourselves on using only the highest quality natural ingredients. Our meals are free from artificial preservatives, colors, and flavors. We use flash-freezing technology to lock in freshness without the need for artificial preservatives.
Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options? +
Absolutely! We offer a wide range of vegetarian and vegan options, clearly labeled in our product catalog. Our plant-based meals are crafted with the same attention to quality and flavor as our meat dishes, ensuring delicious options for all dietary preferences.
How do I track my order? +
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website or the courier's website to track your delivery in real-time. You'll also receive SMS updates at key points during the delivery process.
What if my order arrives damaged? +
We apologize if your order arrives damaged. Please contact our customer service within 24 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged items and packaging. We'll either send a replacement or issue a full refund, depending on your preference and product availability.
Do you offer subscription services? +
Yes, we offer a convenient subscription service that delivers your favorite meals on a regular schedule. Subscribers enjoy 10% off every order, free delivery, and early access to new products. You can customize your delivery frequency and meal selection, and pause or cancel anytime.
